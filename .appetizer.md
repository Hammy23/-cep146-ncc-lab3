# Simple Bruschetta Appetizer 


## Ingredients 
* 1 baguette, sliced 
* 3 tomatoes, diced 
* 2 cloves garlic, minced 
* 2 tbsp olive oil 
* Salt and pepper to taste 
* Fresh basil, chopped 

## Instructions 
1. Preheat oven to 400F.
2. Place baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 mins.
3. In a bowl, mix tomatoes, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and basil.
4. Spoon the mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.
5. Serve immediately
