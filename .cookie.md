# Shortbread Cookies (Precise Recipe)

## Ingredients 
* 2 cups softened butter
* 1 cup white sugar 
* 2 teaspons vanilla extract
* 4 cups all-purpose flour 

## Instructions
1.Prep 
* Preheat oven to 350F (180C)
* Leave baking sheets ungreased 
2.Cream 
* Beat butter + sugar on medium speed until light and fluffy(3-4 mins)
3.Mix
* Stir in vanilla.
* Gradually add flour until a smooth dough forms.
4.Shape 
Choose one method:
* Cookie press: Fill and press onto baking sheets, spacing 1.5 inches apart 
* No press: Roll into 1-inch balls and flatten gently with a fork 
5.Bake 
* Bake 10-12 minutes, until edges just begin turning light golden.
6.Cool
* Let cookies rest on the sheet for a few minutes.
* Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely .

## Notes 
* if dough is too soft: Chill 10 minutes.
* Optional: Dip cooled cookies in melted chocolate or decorate as desired
